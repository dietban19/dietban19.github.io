How to Play
  • Start Typing. The letters will appear in the boxes
  • Remove letters with Backspace

  • Letters with green background are in right spot
  • Letters with yellow background exists in the word, but are in the wrong spots
  • Letters with a gray background does not exist in the word
  • If you need a hint, press the ? button
